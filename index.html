About Me

I’m Vimal, a front-end developer focused on creating beautiful and accessible user interfaces. I enjoy crafting responsive layouts, optimizing performance, and bringing pixel-perfect designs to life.

Curious by nature and committed to growth, I’m always ready to take on new challenges and continue evolving as a developer.
